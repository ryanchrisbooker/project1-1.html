Graphic Designer

Videographer

De'Marcus is one of the more inituitive videographers in the Southern California area. He handles everything from interviewing, editing, filming to sound production. He wears multiple hats as if he has several heads but can easily adapt to collaborate with teams to accomplish the goals of the project.
